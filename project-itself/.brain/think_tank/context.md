# 💡 Think Tank — AI Context

> **Folder Type:** Strategic Planning
> **Audience:** AI Agents

## Purpose
The **strategic growth layer**. This folder stores brainstorms about the project's future direction, architectural evolution possibilities, moonshot ideas, and long-term visionary planning.

## Allowed Contents
- Growth direction proposals (`.md` files)
- Architectural evolution roadmaps
- "What if?" scenario explorations
- Moonshot ideas and experimental feature concepts
- Market opportunity analyses
- Strategic pivot considerations

## Operational Rules
- **No Execution:** Nothing in this folder should be treated as an active task. These are possibilities, not commitments.
- **Structured Brainstorms:** Use a consistent format: Problem → Hypothesis → Proposed Approach → Expected Impact.
- **Linking:** Reference related research from `3_learning/` and tasks from `4_tasks-and-logs/` when proposing ideas.

## AI Guardrails
- **NEVER** start implementing any idea from this folder without the founder's explicit approval and a corresponding task in `4_tasks-and-logs/`.
- **ALWAYS** ground brainstorms in real data — reference research from `3_learning/` whenever possible.
- **ALLOWED** to proactively suggest new ideas here based on patterns observed across the project.
