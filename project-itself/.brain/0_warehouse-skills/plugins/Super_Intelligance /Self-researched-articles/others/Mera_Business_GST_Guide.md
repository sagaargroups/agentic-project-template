# 📌 PART 1 — SABSE PEHLE: BUSINESS KYA HOTA HAI?

## 1.1 Business ki Basic Soch

Business matlab — **kuch dena aur uske badle mein paisa lena.**

Tum ya toh kuch **bechte ho** (Seller / Supplier) ya kuch **khareedte ho** (Buyer / Recipient) — ya dono karte ho. Ek successful business mein yeh dono cheezein balanced honi chahiye.

```
Tumhara Business Flow:
KHAREEDNA (Inward) → KAAM KARNA → BECHNA (Outward) → PAISA AANA → PROFIT
```

## 1.2 Proprietorship Kya Hoti Hai?

**Proprietorship = Tum HI Business ho.**

- Koi partner nahi, koi company nahi
- Saara profit tumhara, saara loss bhi tumhara
- Law ki nazar mein tum aur tumhara business ek hi ho
- Sabse simple aur sabse common business structure India mein
- Koi registration compulsory nahi — lekin GST, bank account, aur income tax ke liye practically zaroori hai

**Proprietorship ke fayde:**
- Kholna aasaan, band karna aasaan
- Kam paperwork
- Saari decision power tumhare haath mein
- Profit seedha tumhari jeb mein

**Proprietorship ke nuksan:**
- Personal aur business liability alag nahi — agar business mein karz ho, to personal property bhi jaati hai
- Bank loan lena thoda mushkil
- Ek insaan ki capacity limited hoti hai

# 📌 PART 2 — PAISON KI BHASHA (ACCOUNTING BASICS)

## 2.1 Har Transaction Ke 2 Pehlu Hote Hain

Yeh sabse important concept hai jo tumhe samajhna hai agle sab kuch samajhne se pehle.

**Jab bhi kuch hota hai business mein — ek jagah se kuch JAATA hai, doosri jagah kuch AATA hai.**

```
Example:
Tumne ₹10,000 ka samaan kharida aur cash diya.
→ Samaan (Stock) AAYA tumhare paas  ✅
→ Cash GAYA tumse  ❌

Tumne ₹15,000 ka samaan becha aur cash mila.
→ Cash AAYA tumhare paas  ✅
→ Samaan (Stock) GAYA tumse  ❌
```

## 2.2 Debit aur Credit — Seedha Samjho

Yeh words log zyada complicated banate hain. Simple version:

| Concept | Simple Matlab |
|---------|--------------|
| **Debit (Dr)** | Jo AAYA — Asset badha, Ya expense hua |
| **Credit (Cr)** | Jo GAYA — Asset ghata, Ya income aayi |

> **Bank wala ulta bolte hain kyun?** Jab bank tumhare account mein "credited" bolte hain, matlab bank ne APNI liability badhaayi — kyunki woh tumhara paisa hold kar rahe hain. Tumhare liye woh ASSET hai. Bank ke liye LIABILITY. Isliye confusing lagta hai. Business accounting mein apne perspective se socho.

## 2.3 Important Financial Terms

**Turnover** — Ek saal mein total kitna BECHA? Profit nahi, sirf bikri ka total. Agar tumne ₹50 lakh ka samaan becha, turnover ₹50 lakh hai.

**Revenue** — Business mein aane wala paisa — sales se.

**Expense** — Business ka kharcha — rent, bijli, maal khareedna, salary.

**Profit** = Revenue − Expenses

**Loss** = Expenses > Revenue

**Capital** — Tumhara apna lagaya hua paisa ya asset jo business shuru karne ke liye laga.

**Asset** — Jo tumhare paas hai aur uski value hai. Cash, machine, stock, computer.

**Liability** — Jo tumne kisi ka DENA hai. Bank loan, unpaid bills, GST payable.

**Cash Flow** — Actual cash ka andar-bahar aana. Business profitable ho sakta hai kagaz pe, lekin cash nahi ho — yeh hamesha dhyan rakho.

**Ledger** — Har ek account ki apni "register" — jaise ek khaata. Tally ya kisi bhi software mein yeh automatically banta hai.

**Balance Sheet** — Ek date pe tumhara poora financial picture:
```
ASSETS (jo tumhara hai) = LIABILITIES (jo tumhara uthana hai) + CAPITAL (tumhara hissa)
```

**Profit & Loss Statement (P&L)** — Ek period mein kitna kamaya, kitna kharch kiya, kitna bacha.

# 📌 PART 3 — DOCUMENTS JO TUMHE ROZ KAAM AAYENGE

## 3.1 Invoice (Bill) — Sabse Zaroori Document

Invoice ek **legal document** hai jo seller banata hai aur buyer ko deta hai. Iska matlab hai:

> "Maine tumhe yeh cheez bachi, itne mein, aaj ki date pe — ab mujhe paisa do."

**Invoice mein kya hona chahiye (GST ke saath):**

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│           TAX INVOICE                       │
│                                             │
│ Seller Name & Address                       │
│ Seller GSTIN                                │
│ Invoice Number (unique, sequential)         │
│ Invoice Date                                │
│                                             │
│ Buyer Name & Address                        │
│ Buyer GSTIN (agar registered hai)           │
│ Place of Supply (state)                     │
│                                             │
│ Item | HSN Code | Qty | Rate | Amount       │
│ ─────────────────────────────────────────── │
│ Sub Total                                   │
│ CGST @9%                                   │
│ SGST @9%                                   │
│ OR IGST @18%                               │
│ ─────────────────────────────────────────── │
│ TOTAL AMOUNT                               │
│                                             │
│ Amount in Words                             │
│ Signature / Stamp                           │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Invoice Number kaise rakho:**
Ek financial year mein ek sequence follow karo. Jaise: `INV-2025-001`, `INV-2025-002`... Kabhi bhi number repeat mat karo.

## 3.2 Bill of Supply

Jab tum GST NAHI charge karte — tab invoice ki jagah **Bill of Supply** dete ho.

Kab use hota hai:
- Tum exempt goods bech rahe ho (jaise fresh vegetables)
- Tum Composition Scheme mein ho
- Export (zero-rated)

Bill of Supply mein GST amount nahi likhte — sirf value likhte ho.

## 3.3 Credit Note — "Minus Karna"

Agar tumne invoice raise kiya, lekin baad mein:
- Customer ne maal wapas kiya
- Tumne zyada charge kar diya
- Maal damaged tha
- Discount baad mein agree hua

Tab tum **Credit Note** bnate ho — jo uss invoice ki value ko **reduce** karta hai.

```
Original Invoice: ₹10,000 + GST ₹1,800 = ₹11,800
Customer ne ₹2,000 ka maal wapas kiya.
Credit Note: ₹2,000 + GST ₹360 = ₹2,360 (reduce hua)
Net payable: ₹11,800 − ₹2,360 = ₹9,440
```

**GST mein:** Credit Note se tumhari Output Tax Liability GHATTI hai.

## 3.4 Debit Note — "Plus Karna"

Agar invoice raise karne ke baad:
- Tumne kam charge kiya tha
- Extra maal gaya jo bill nahi hua
- Price baad mein badhna tha

Tab **Debit Note** banate ho — jo amount **badhata** hai.

> **Yaad rakhne ka trick:**
> - Credit Note = Customer ko credit do = Unka bill GHATA
> - Debit Note = Customer ko debit karo = Unka bill BADHA

## 3.5 Purchase Order (PO)

BUYER banata hai SELLER ko — KHAREEDNE SE PEHLE.

Iska matlab: "Mujhe yeh cheezein chahiye, is rate pe, is date tak — kya tum de sakte ho?"

Seller agar agree kare, to PO ke against maal bhejta hai aur invoice raise karta hai.

Bade businesses mein PO mandatory hota hai. Proprietorship mein aata-jaata hai, lekin organized rahne ke liye use karo.

## 3.6 Delivery Challan

Jab maal jaaye lekin invoice abhi nahi — tab Delivery Challan use karte hain.

Kab use hota hai:
- Maal job work ke liye bheja (processing ke liye)
- Branch transfer (apni hi doosri jagah)
- Approval pe maal bheja (customer dekhega, paise baad mein)
- Free sample

Delivery Challan pe GST nahi hota — woh sirf "yeh maal ja raha hai" ka record hai.

## 3.7 Receipt Voucher

Jab customer ADVANCE paisa de — tab Receipt Voucher banate ho. Invoice tab banta hai jab maal ya service dete ho. GST bhi tab lagta hai.

## 3.8 Payment Voucher

Jab tum kisi ko paisa do — tab Payment Voucher banate ho apne records mein.

## 3.9 E-Way Bill

₹50,000 se zyada ka maal transport karo (ek jagah se doosri jagah) — E-Way Bill zaroori hai.

- GST portal pe generate hota hai: `ewaybillgst.nic.in`
- Ek unique EWB number milta hai
- Driver ke paas hona chahiye physically ya digitally
- Validity: 1 din per 200 km (approximately)
- Bina EWB ke maal pakda gaya = penalty + maal seized

# 📌 PART 4 — GST KA POORA SAMAJH

## 4.1 GST Kya Hai — Ek Baar Aur, Seedha

GST = Goods and Services Tax

Tumhara samaan ya service jab bhi BIKTA hai — government uska ek hissa chahti hai. Woh hissa GST hai. Tum customer se GST collect karte ho aur government ko de dete ho. Lekin jo tumne KHAREEDNE pe diya tha, woh wapas milta hai — **yahi ITC hai.**

## 4.2 GST Ke Types

| Type | Full Form | Kab Lagta Hai | Kise Jaata Hai |
|------|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| **CGST** | Central GST | Same state mein sale | Central Govt |
| **SGST** | State GST | Same state mein sale | State Govt |
| **IGST** | Integrated GST | Different state mein sale | Central Govt (baad mein divide) |
| **UTGST** | Union Territory GST | UT mein sale | UT Administration |

```
Example:
Tum Gujarat mein ho, customer bhi Gujarat mein.
Sale ₹1,00,000 at 18% GST:
→ CGST 9% = ₹9,000 (Centre ko)
→ SGST 9% = ₹9,000 (Gujarat Govt ko)

Tum Gujarat mein ho, customer Maharashtra mein.
Sale ₹1,00,000 at 18% GST:
→ IGST 18% = ₹18,000 (Centre ko — baad mein states mein divide hoga)
```

## 4.3 GST Rates — Kya Kitne Pe Lagta Hai

| Rate | Kya Aata Hai |
|------|-------------|
| **0%** | Rice, wheat, milk, fresh vegetables, eggs, books, healthcare |
| **5%** | Edible oil, sugar, tea, coffee, economy class flights, small restaurants |
| **12%** | Butter, cheese, fruit juice, business class, some medicines |
| **18%** | Most services, electronics, AC restaurants, software, construction services |
| **28%** | Luxury cars, tobacco, aerated drinks, luxury hotels, casinos |

**Kaise pata kare kaunsa rate lagta hai?**
HSN code dhundo (goods ke liye) ya SAC code (services ke liye) — rate automatically pata chal jaata hai.

## 4.4 HSN Code aur SAC Code

**HSN Code** (Harmonized System of Nomenclature) — Goods ke liye ek unique number.
**SAC Code** (Services Accounting Code) — Services ke liye unique number.

Yeh codes tumhare invoice pe likhne zaroori hain. Yeh internationally standardized hain.

```
Example HSN Codes:
1006 = Rice
6109 = T-shirts, vests
8471 = Computers

Example SAC Codes:
9954 = Construction services
9983 = IT services
9971 = Financial services
```

Turnover ₹5 crore tak = 4-digit HSN sufficient
₹5 crore se zyada = 6-digit HSN compulsory

**HSN kaise dhundho:** `gst.gov.in` pe HSN search tool hai. Ya simply Google karo: "HSN code for [your product]"

## 4.5 Input Tax Credit (ITC) — GST Ka Dil

Yeh GST ka SABSE important concept hai. Agar yeh samajh gaye, baaki sab aasaan hai.

**Simple Logic:**
```
Tum ₹100 ka maal khareedne pe ₹18 GST dete ho (18%)
Phir tum woh maal ₹150 mein bechte ho aur ₹27 GST lete ho

Government ko sirf difference dena hai:
₹27 (Output Tax) - ₹18 (Input Tax Credit) = ₹9 sirf yeh dena hai

Baaki ₹18 jo tumne pehle diya tha — woh "credit" mein adjust ho gaya.
```

**ITC claim karne ki conditions:**
1. Tumhare paas supplier ki **valid Tax Invoice** honi chahiye
2. Tumne maal/service **actually receive** ki ho
3. Supplier ne woh GST **government ko** file kiya ho (GSTR-1 mein show ho)
4. Maal/service **business use** ke liye ho (personal use ke liye ITC nahi milta)
5. Tumhari GST return filed ho

**ITC kab NAHI milta:**
- Personal use ke items pe (car for personal use, food, travel)
- Construction of immovable property pe (apni building banane pe)
- Motor vehicles pe (kuch exceptions hain)
- Items jo exempt goods/services banane mein use hote hain

## 4.6 GST Registration

**Kab register karna zaroori hai:**
- Goods ka business: Annual turnover > ₹40 lakh
- Services ka business: Annual turnover > ₹20 lakh
- Special category states (Northeast, Uttarakhand): > ₹10 lakh
- Inter-state supply karte ho: Registration mandatory regardless of turnover
- E-commerce platform pe bechte ho: Mandatory

**Voluntarily register bhi kar sakte ho** — fayda: ITC milega, credibility badhi.

**Registration process:**
1. `gst.gov.in` pe jaao
2. "New Registration" choose karo
3. PAN, Aadhaar, mobile, email se start
4. Business details bharo
5. Documents upload karo
6. ARN milega (Application Reference Number)
7. 7 working days mein GSTIN milega

**Documents needed:**
- PAN Card (proprietor ka)
- Aadhaar Card
- Business address proof (electricity bill / rent agreement)
- Bank account proof (cancelled cheque / statement)
- Passport size photo
- Business name ka koi proof (if any)

**GSTIN Structure — 15 digits:**
```
27 AAAAA0000A 1 Z 5
│  │          │ │ │
│  │          │ │ └── Check digit
│  │          │ └──── Default 'Z'
│  │          └────── Entity code (1st registration = 1)
│  └───────────────── PAN (10 digits)
└──────────────────── State Code (27 = Maharashtra, 24 = Gujarat)
```

## 4.7 Composition Scheme — Chote Business Ke Liye

Agar tumhara turnover ₹1.5 crore se kam hai, tum Composition Scheme le sakte ho.

**Fayde:**
- Bahut kam rate pay karo (Goods: 1%, Restaurants: 5%, Others: 6%)
- Simple returns — quarter mein sirf ek baar
- Less paperwork

**Nuksan:**
- ITC claim nahi kar sakte
- Inter-state supply nahi kar sakte
- Customer se GST separately charge nahi kar sakte
- Invoice pe "Composition Taxable Person" likhna padta hai

**Suitable for:** Local small shops, small restaurants, local service providers.

**Not suitable for:** Koi bhi jo inter-state bechta ho, ya jise ITC claim karni ho.

# 📌 PART 5 — GST RETURNS — GOVERNMENT KO REPORT KARNA

## 5.1 Returns Ka Simple Matlab

Return = Government ko batana ki tumne kitna becha, kitna kharida, aur kitna tax diya/liya.

Yeh ek periodic report hai jo **online file** hoti hai GST portal pe.

## 5.2 Tumhare Liye Important Returns

### GSTR-1 — "Maine kitna becha"
- **Kya hai:** Tumhari SAARI outward supplies (sales) ki detail
- **Kab file hoti hai:** Monthly — 11th of next month (agar turnover > ₹5 crore) | Quarterly — QRMP scheme ke liye
- **Kya hota hai isse:** Tumhare buyer ka GSTR-2B automatically update hota hai — unhe ITC milti hai

### GSTR-3B — "Mujhe kitna pay karna hai"
- **Kya hai:** Summary return + actual tax payment
- **Kab file hoti hai:** Monthly — 20th of next month
- **Kya hota hai:** Yahan Output Tax minus ITC = Net Tax pay karte ho
- **Important:** Yeh khud manually fill karna padta hai, galti mat karna

### GSTR-2B — "Mujhe kitna ITC milega"
- **Kya hai:** Auto-generated statement — suppliers ne jo GSTR-1 mein dala, woh yahan show hota hai
- **Tumhara kaam:** Is statement se apni purchase register match karo — jo match kare woh ITC lo

### GSTR-9 — "Saal bhar ka hisaab"
- **Kya hai:** Annual return — poore saal ka summary
- **Kab file hoti hai:** 31st December of next financial year
- **Kya hota hai:** Saari monthly returns ka reconciliation

### GSTR-4 — Composition Scheme walon ke liye
- Quarterly file hoti hai — 18th of next month after quarter

## 5.3 Return Filing Ka Flow — Har Mahine Kya Karo

```
Month End hone ke baad:

STEP 1 (1st–10th):
Apni saari sales ke invoices check karo.
GSTR-1 file karo — 11th tak.

STEP 2 (14th ke baad):
GSTR-2B dekho — suppliers ne kya file kiya.
Apni purchase invoices se match karo.
Jo match kare = ITC claim karo.
Jo match na kare = Supplier se poochho.

STEP 3 (20th tak):
GSTR-3B file karo.
Output Tax (sales pe GST) - ITC = Net Tax
Net Tax pay karo — electronic credit ledger se ya bank se.
```

## 5.4 GST Payment — Kaise Hota Hai

GST teen tarike se pay hota hai (teen "ledgers" hain portal pe):

**Electronic Cash Ledger:** Real paisa — tumhara bank se portal pe NEFT/UPI se daala hua.

**Electronic Credit Ledger:** ITC ka balance — jo tax tumne khareedari pe diya tha.

**Electronic Liability Ledger:** Jo tumhara tax banta hai — government ko dena hai.

```
Payment flow:
ITC (Credit Ledger) se pehle adjust karo liability.
Jo bachti hai woh Cash Ledger se pay karo.
```

# 📌 PART 6 — PLACE OF SUPPLY aur TIME OF SUPPLY

## 6.1 Place of Supply — Bahut Important

**Yeh decide karta hai ki CGST+SGST lagega ya IGST.**

**Goods ke liye:**
- Jahan maal actually DELIVER hua = Place of Supply
- Same state delivery = CGST + SGST
- Different state delivery = IGST

**Services ke liye:**
- Usually buyer ki location = Place of Supply
- Kuch services ke liye alag rules hain (real estate, transportation, etc.)

```
Tum Ahmedabad mein ho.
Customer Surat mein = Gujarat → CGST + SGST (same state)
Customer Pune mein = Maharashtra → IGST (different state)
Customer Dubai mein = Export → IGST @ 0% (zero rated)
```

## 6.2 Time of Supply — Kab Tax Banta Hai

**Goods ke liye:**
Invoice date aur delivery date — jo PEHLE ho woh = Tax banta hai

**Services ke liye:**
Invoice date aur payment date — jo PEHLE ho woh = Tax banta hai

```
Example:
Tumne service 1st March ko ki.
Invoice 5th March ko diya.
Payment 10th March ko aaya.

Time of Supply = 5th March (Invoice date pehle aaya)
March ki return mein yeh show hoga.
```

# 📌 PART 7 — SPECIAL SITUATIONS JO TUMHARE KAAM AAYENGI

## 7.1 Reverse Charge Mechanism (RCM)

Normal mein: **Seller GST pay karta hai.**
RCM mein: **Buyer GST pay karta hai — directly government ko.**

**Kab hota hai:**
- Unregistered supplier se khareedna (specific goods/services mein)
- Specific services jaise: Advocate services, GTA (Goods Transport Agency), import of services
- E-commerce operators pe listed freelancers se services

**Tumhara action RCM mein:**
1. Supplier se invoice lo bina GST ke
2. Khud se RCM invoice banao
3. GST calculate karo aur pay karo cash mein
4. Same GST ka ITC bhi usually le sakte ho (agar eligible ho)
5. GSTR-3B mein dono show karo

## 7.2 Export — Zero Rated Supply

Agar goods ya services **India se bahar** bhejte ho — yeh EXPORT hai.

GST 0% lagta hai BUT tum ITC claim kar sakte ho.

**Do options:**
1. **LUT (Letter of Undertaking)** — Bina GST diye export karo aur ITC refund lo
2. **With IGST** — Export pe IGST pay karo, phir refund claim karo

LUT recommended hai — cash flow better rehta hai.

## 7.3 Exempt Supply

Kuch goods/services pe GST bilkul nahi — na collect karo, na ITC lo.

```
Examples:
- Fresh vegetables, fruits, milk
- Healthcare services
- Educational services
- Residential rent (kuch conditions mein)
```

Agar sirf exempt supply karte ho — registration ki zaroorat nahi (mostly).
Agar mixed supply (exempt + taxable) — tab ITC proportionately milti hai.

## 7.4 Mixed Supply aur Composite Supply

**Composite Supply:** Ek main supply ke saath naturally linked cheez. Jaise: Mobile phone ke saath charger bhi aaya. Main supply (mobile) ka rate lagega dono pe.

**Mixed Supply:** Alag-alag items ek saath bech rahe ho jo naturally linked nahi hain. Jaise: Gift hamper mein chocolates, drinks, aur toys. Sabse ZYADA rate wali item ka rate sab pe lagega.

# 📌 PART 9 — BANK ACCOUNT aur PAISON KA MANAGEMENT

## 9.1 Business Account MUST Hai

Proprietorship ke liye **Current Account** khulwao — kisi bhi bank mein.

**Kyun:**
- GST refund is account mein aata hai
- GST payment is account se hota hai
- Professional credibility
- Business transactions track karna aasaan
- Income tax ke time clarity hogi

**Current Account ke liye:**
- PAN
- Aadhaar
- Business proof (GST certificate, ya shop act, ya udyam)
- Address proof

## 9.2 Cash aur Digital — Balance Rakho

₹2 lakh se zyada cash mein LENA mat — income tax issue ho sakta hai.
₹10,000 se zyada cash mein expenses mat karo ek din mein business mein — ITC nahi milega.

UPI, NEFT, RTGS, cheque — yeh use karo. Paper trail rehti hai.

# 📌 PART 10 — INCOME TAX aur GST KA RELATION

Bahut log confuse ho jaate hain — yeh alag hain, lekin related hain.

| | **GST** | **Income Tax** |
|---|---------|----------------|
| **Kya hai** | Sales pe tax | Profit/Income pe tax |
| **Kab dena hai** | Monthly/Quarterly | Annually (Advance quarterly) |
| **Kaun deta hai** | Customer actually, tum collect karte ho | Tum apni kamaai pe |
| **Return** | GSTR-1, 3B, 9 | ITR-3 (Proprietorship ke liye) |
| **Department** | GST Council / GSTN | Income Tax Dept |

**Proprietorship ke liye Income Tax:**
- Tumhara business income = Tumhara personal income
- ITR-3 file karte hain
- Business ka profit tumhari income mein add hota hai
- Applicable slab rates lagti hain

**Advance Tax:** Agar tumhara annual tax ₹10,000 se zyada banta hai — teen installments mein advance mein pay karna padta hai.

# 📌 PART 11 — NOTICES, AUDIT, aur COMPLIANCE

## 11.1 GST Notice Kab Aata Hai

- Return file nahi ki
- GSTR-1 aur 3B mein mismatch
- GSTR-2B se tumhari ITC claim match nahi karti
- Suspicious transactions
- Scrutiny assessment

## 11.2 Notice Aaye Toh Kya Karo

1. **Ghabrao mat** — notice = opportunity to explain
2. Notice ध्यान se padho — kaunsa section, kya issue
3. Timeline note karo — reply ki deadline
4. CA ya GST Practitioner se help lo
5. Portal pe login karke reply submit karo
6. Saare documents ready rakho

## 11.3 GST Audit

Agar turnover ₹2 crore se zyada — GST Audit mandatory tha (ab yeh rules change hue hain, check karo latest).

CA se audit karana hota hai. Saari books ready rakhni padti hain.

## 11.4 Penalties Se Kaise Bcho

| Galti | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| Return late file karna | ₹50/day (CGST ₹25 + SGST ₹25) |
| Tax late pay karna | 18% annual interest |
| Fake ITC lena | 100% penalty |
| E-Way Bill nahi | ₹10,000 ya tax amount (jo zyada ho) |
| Non-registration | 10% of tax due, minimum ₹10,000 |

**Bachne ka tarika:** Time pe return bharo, invoices sahi rakho, ITC sirf valid purchases pe lo.

# 📌 PART 15 — COMMON MISTAKES JO MAT KARNA

❌ **Supplier ka GSTIN verify nahi kiya** — Fake GSTIN pe ITC nahi milega

❌ **Invoice number ek baar se zyada use kiya** — Illegal hai, notice aayega

❌ **Cash mein ₹10,000+ ka payment kiya business expense ke liye** — ITC block ho jaayegi

❌ **Return late file ki** — Penalty + Interest dono lagenge

❌ **GSTR-1 aur 3B mein different figures daali** — Mismatch notice aayega

❌ **Personal aur business expenses mix kiye** — Tax aur audit dono ka problem

❌ **E-Way Bill nahi banaya** — Maal seized ho sakta hai, hefty penalty

❌ **ITC liya without proper invoice** — Fraud mana jaata hai, 100% penalty

❌ **Business account nahi khola** — Transactions track nahi honge, problems aayengi

❌ **Kisi bhi notice ko ignore kiya** — Ex-parte order aa jaata hai, bank account freeze
