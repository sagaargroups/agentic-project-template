# Headshot & Portrait Prompts

## Professional Business Headshot

```
Professional business headshot of a [gender] [profession] in their [age range].
Wearing: [business casual/formal suit/smart casual].
Expression: confident, approachable, warm smile.
Background: neutral gray or soft gradient.
Lighting: professional studio, flattering.
Suitable for LinkedIn, company website, business cards.
```

## Tech Professional

```
Modern professional headshot of a [gender] tech professional.
Wearing: casual smart attire (no tie).
Setting: modern office or co-working space background.
Expression: friendly, innovative, approachable.
Lighting: natural with fill light.
Style: contemporary, Silicon Valley vibe.
```

## Speaker/Author

```
Professional author/speaker portrait of a [gender] expert.
Pose: confident, facing camera, slight angle.
Expression: thoughtful, authoritative yet approachable.
Background: dark or blurred professional setting.
Lighting: dramatic but flattering.
Suitable for book covers, speaking pages.
```

## Lifestyle/Personal Brand

```
Lifestyle portrait of a [gender] [profession/coach/influencer].
Setting: [office/outdoors/modern space].
Activity: [working/speaking/casual pose].
Expression: authentic, engaging, relatable.
Style: editorial, magazine quality.
Natural lighting with depth of field.
```

## Team Photo (Individual)

```
Corporate team member portrait of a [gender] [role].
Wearing: company-appropriate attire.
Background: consistent neutral color for team page.
Expression: professional, friendly.
Lighting: even, studio quality.
Format: square crop for website grid.
```

## Variables

| Variable | Options |
|----------|---------|
| [gender] | man, woman, person |
| [age range] | 20s, 30s, 40s, 50s |
| [profession] | developer, designer, CEO, consultant |
| [attire] | business casual, formal, smart casual |

## Tips

- Always specify "professional" for quality
- Include lighting preferences
- Mention intended use (LinkedIn, website)
- Specify background preference
- Add "high resolution, 4K" for quality
