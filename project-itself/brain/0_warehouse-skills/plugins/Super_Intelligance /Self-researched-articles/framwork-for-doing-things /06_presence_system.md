## CPS Engine A: What's Already Proven About Presence

> *"Who has already mastered presence? What structures can we copy?"*

### The 4 Layers (Source A — Proven Structure)

```
LAYER 1: PHYSICAL PRESENCE
  │  You exist in their physical space
  │
LAYER 2: DIGITAL PRESENCE
  │  You exist in their digital space
  │
LAYER 3: MENTAL PRESENCE
  │  You exist in their MIND — they think of you when the need arises
  │
LAYER 4: EMOTIONAL PRESENCE
     You exist in their HEART — they feel connected, loyal, bonded
```

---

### Layer 1: Physical Presence

> *"The handshake layer. The oldest form of presence."*

| Proven Strategy | How It Works | Who Does It Best |
|---|---|---|
| **Events & Conferences** | Being physically where your audience gathers | Apple keynotes, TED, industry trade shows |
| **Retail & Pop-ups** | Tangible experience of your brand | Apple Store (you TOUCH the product), pop-up restaurants |
| **Face-to-face networking** | 1-on-1 trust building — nothing digital replaces this | Every major deal in history started with a handshake |
| **Physical collateral** | Business cards, packaging, merchandise — tactile memory | Unboxing experiences (Apple, luxury brands) |

**The Proven Rule:**
```
Physical presence builds TRUST faster than any digital channel.
One 30-minute face-to-face meeting = 100 social media posts in trust-building.
But it doesn't SCALE. That's what Layer 2 is for.
```

---

### Layer 2: Digital Presence

> *"The scale layer. Reach millions. But depth is shallow unless layered with L3-L4."*

| Proven Strategy | How It Works | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| **Search (SEO)** | When they search, YOU appear. Intent-based presence | Page 1 = exists. Page 2 = invisible |
| **Social Media** | Where they scroll, you show up. Attention-based presence | Consistency > virality. 1 post/day for 365 days > 1 viral post |
| **Content** | Teach, entertain, or solve — and your name is attached | The VALUE of the content determines if presence converts to trust |
| **Paid Ads** | Buy attention in channels where your audience lives | Ads without perception strategy = burning money for noise |
| **Email / Direct** | YOU control this channel — no algorithm between you and them | The only channel you OWN. Everything else is rented |

**The Proven Rule:**
```
Own 1-2 channels DEEPLY before adding more.
Thin presence across 7 channels = invisible everywhere.
Deep presence in 1 channel = known, trusted, remembered.
```

---

### Layer 3: Mental Presence (The Most Valuable Layer)

> *"When the need arises, whose name surfaces in their mind FIRST? That person wins."*

| Proven Strategy | How It Works | Time to Build |
|---|---|---|
| **Category Ownership** | Be known for ONE thing. "X is THE person/brand for Y" | 6-12 months of consistent positioning |
| **Repetition with value** | Show up consistently, every time delivering something useful | 50+ valuable touchpoints before mental real estate is claimed |
| **Distinctive assets** | Unique colors, sounds, phrases, visuals that ONLY you use | These become neural shortcuts — brain recognizes you in 0.5 seconds |
| **Association triggers** | Link yourself to a moment, problem, or emotion | "When I feel [X], I think of [your brand]" |

**The Proven Rule:**
```
Mental Presence follows the Rule of 7×7:
  → 7+ encounters before they REMEMBER you
  → 7+ valuable encounters before they TRUST you
  → Consistency × Distinctiveness × Value = Mental Real Estate

Note: The brain DELETES the generic. It KEEPS the distinctive.
If you look/sound like everyone else, you are neurologically invisible.
```

---

### Layer 4: Emotional Presence (The Unbreakable Layer)

> *"They don't just know you. They FEEL something for you. This is loyalty beyond logic."*

| Proven Strategy | How It Works | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Shared values** | Stand FOR something. People bond with aligned values | Patagonia ("We're in business to save the planet") |
| **Vulnerability** | Show the human behind the brand — struggles, failures, real stories | Founders who share the REAL journey, not just the highlight reel |
| **Community** | Create a space where your audience connects with EACH OTHER (not just you) | Harley-Davidson (riders community), CrossFit (box culture) |
| **Surprise & delight** | Unexpected value beyond what they paid for | Handwritten thank-you notes, unexpected bonuses, remembering their name |

**The Proven Rule:**
```
Emotional Presence cannot be BOUGHT. It can only be EARNED.
It requires:
  → Time (months to years of consistent authentic behavior)
  → Authenticity (one fake move destroys years of emotional presence)
  → Reciprocity (they must feel you care about THEM, not just their money)
```

## CPS Engine B: Experimentation for Presence

> *"What UNKNOWNS about presence could kill your strategy? Hunt them in Week 1."*

### Unknown Mapping (Specific to Presence)

| Unknown | Why It Can Kill You | Micro-Experiment (≤ 1 week) |
|---|---|---|
| "Am I in the RIGHT channel?" | Being present where your audience ISN'T = invisible despite effort | Post the same content on 3 different channels for 7 days. Measure response. Let data decide, not assumptions |
| "Is my content building trust or just attention?" | Attention ≠ presence. Viral but forgotten = wasted effort | Ask 10 followers: "What do you associate me with?" Their answer reveals your REAL mental presence |
| "Am I distinctive enough?" | If you look like everyone else, the brain deletes you | Show your content mixed with 5 competitors' content to someone new. "Which one stands out?" If they can't tell you apart, you're invisible |
| "Is my consistency sufficient?" | Sporadic presence = rebuilding from zero each time | Track your posting/showing-up cadence. If gaps > 7 days, mental presence is decaying |
| "Am I building the right LAYER?" | Spending all effort on L1-L2 when L3-L4 is where the value lives | Survey: "If you needed [your category] tomorrow, whose name comes to mind first?" If not yours → L3 is missing |

## CPS Questioning System Applied to Presence

### Before Building Presence
```
DIAGNOSTIC:
□ "Where am I ACTUALLY present right now — honestly?"
□ "Which layer am I strongest at? Which is zero?"
□ "Where is my audience ACTUALLY spending their time? (Data, not assumption)"

DEPTH:
□ "WHY would someone remember me after encountering me once?"
□ "What is the ONE thing I want to own in their mind?"

INVERSION:
□ "How would I GUARANTEE that nobody remembers me?"
  → Being generic, inconsistent, present in wrong channels, giving no value
□ "Am I accidentally doing any of those things right now?"

ADJACENT:
□ "How does a MUSICIAN build presence? What can I steal from that?"
□ "How does a RELIGION build presence? What structures can I borrow?"
```

### During Presence-Building
```
□ "Is this touchpoint building MENTAL presence or just DIGITAL presence?"
□ "If I stopped showing up for 30 days, would they notice? Would they care?"
□ "Am I repeating or am I deepening? (Showing the same thing vs revealing new layers)"
```

### After (Review)
```
□ "Can 5 random followers tell me what I'm known for in 1 sentence?"
□ "Which layer grew this month? Which stagnated?"
□ "What surprised me about where my audience actually engages?"
```

## CPS 7 Laws Applied to Presence

| Law | Presence Application |
|---|---|
| **Inversion** | List everything that makes a brand forgettable. Then check: am I doing any of those? |
| **Speed of Iteration** | Don't plan content for 3 months. Post for 7 days, measure, adjust, repeat. Fastest tester wins |
| **Constraint Paradox** | "I can only use 1 channel for 90 days." This constraint forces you to go DEEP instead of wide. Deep > wide for presence |
| **Adjacent Possible** | How does a STREET PERFORMER build instant presence? Eye contact, immediate value, distinctiveness, crowd momentum. Apply these to digital |
| **Antifragility** | A public mistake, handled with grace, INCREASES your presence and trust. Don't hide from criticism — convert it |
| **Barbell** | 90% of presence effort on your proven best channel. 10% on one wild experiment (new platform, unexpected format, bold move) |
| **Map ≠ Territory** | Your analytics dashboard is the MAP. Your audience's actual experience is the TERRITORY. Talk to real humans, not just metrics |

## Presence Execution Protocol

### Pre-Flight (Before Building Presence)
```
□ P01. I know exactly which LAYER I'm building (Physical / Digital / Mental / Emotional)
□ P02. I know exactly WHERE my audience spends time (data-backed, not assumed)
□ P03. I have chosen 1-2 channels to own DEEPLY (not 7 channels thinly)
□ P04. I can state what I want to OWN in their mind in 1 sentence
□ P05. I have a distinctive asset (visual, verbal, or format) that ONLY I use
□ P06. I have listed 3 unknowns and designed experiments for each
□ P07. I have answered: "How would I guarantee being forgettable?" and checked I'm not doing those things
□ P08. My first 7 days of presence are planned and ready to execute
□ P09. I have a value-first approach — every touchpoint teaches, solves, or moves them
□ P10. I have a consistency plan I can sustain for 90+ days without burnout
□ P11. My barbell is set: 90% proven channel + 10% experimental
□ P12. I have at least 1 cross-domain idea borrowed for my presence strategy
```

### In-Flight (While Building Presence)
```
□ P13. I am showing up at my committed cadence — no gaps > 7 days
□ P14. I am measuring: which touchpoints generate ENGAGEMENT vs just VIEWS?
□ P15. I have asked 5+ people: "What do you associate me with?" and compared to my intended positioning
□ P16. My experiments are running — I'm not waiting for "more followers before testing"
□ P17. I am deepening (revealing new layers) not just repeating (same content recycled)
□ P18. I am tracking which LAYER is growing (not just follower count)
□ P19. When data says a channel isn't working, I pivot within 7 days
□ P20. I am documenting what works AND what fails
□ P21. I handled at least 1 negative interaction with grace (antifragility)
□ P22. I did at least 1 unexpected/bold thing this month (10% barbell)
```

### Post-Flight (Presence Review — Monthly)
```
□ P23. MENTAL PRESENCE CHECK: "If my audience needs [my category] today, do they think of me FIRST?"
□ P24. EMOTIONAL PRESENCE CHECK: "Would they notice and CARE if I disappeared for 30 days?"
□ P25. Which layer grew most? Which needs attention?
□ P26. What surprised me? (Surprises = gaps in my understanding)
□ P27. What experiment taught me the most?
□ P28. System updated: new learnings integrated, failed approaches pruned
□ P29. Benchmark raised: presence standard for next month is HIGHER
□ P30. Iteration speed: am I testing FASTER than last month?
```
