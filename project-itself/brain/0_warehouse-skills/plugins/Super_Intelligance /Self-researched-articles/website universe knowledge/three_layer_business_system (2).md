# LAYER 1: FIND вҶ’ GET вҶ’ DISTRIBUTE

## The Core Business Model

### 1. FIND (Finding Clients & Freelancers)

**FIND CLIENTS:**
- Local businesses (walk-in, WhatsApp, Instagram)
- Online leads (website form, Google Business, social media)
- Referrals (existing clients)

**FIND FREELANCERS:**
- Upwork, Fiverr, Freelancer.com
- Local developers/designers
- Student interns (colleges)
- Online communities (Reddit, Facebook groups)

**Zoho System:**
```
Clients вҶ’ Zoho CRM (as Leads/Customers)
Freelancers вҶ’ Zoho People (as Contractors/Vendors)
```

---

### 2. GET (Getting Projects & Resources)

**GET PROJECTS FROM CLIENTS:**
- Discovery call вҶ’ Proposal вҶ’ Contract вҶ’ Deposit вҶ’ Project starts

**GET WORK DONE:**
- Small project (<вӮ№50K): You do it
- Medium project (вӮ№50K-вӮ№1L): You + 1 freelancer
- Large project (>вӮ№1L): You + 2-3 freelancers

**Zoho System:**
```
Won Deal (CRM) вҶ’ Creates Project (Zoho Projects)
Assign Tasks вҶ’ To You OR To Freelancer
Track Progress вҶ’ Real-time in Projects
```

---

### 3. DISTRIBUTE (Distributing Work & Payment)

**DISTRIBUTE WORK TO FREELANCERS:**

**Example: вӮ№80,000 Website Project**

```
YOU handle:
- Client communication (100% your role)
- Requirements gathering
- Project management
- Quality control
- Client handover

FREELANCER 1 (Designer) handles:
- Website design/mockup
- Payment: вӮ№15,000

FREELANCER 2 (Developer) handles:
- WordPress development
- Payment: вӮ№20,000

FREELANCER 3 (Content Writer) handles:
- Content writing (if needed)
- Payment: вӮ№5,000

YOUR EARNINGS:
Revenue: вӮ№80,000
- Freelancer costs: -вӮ№40,000
- Tools/hosting: -вӮ№3,000
- Your profit: вӮ№37,000
- Your time: 10-15 hours (vs. 30 hours doing it yourself)
```

**DISTRIBUTE PAYMENTS:**

**Via Zoho:**
```
Client pays you вҶ’ Zoho Invoice (вӮ№80,000)
вҶ“
Record in Zoho Books (Income)
вҶ“
You pay freelancers вҶ’ Zoho Expense (вӮ№15K, вӮ№20K, вӮ№5K)
вҶ“
Net profit auto-calculated вҶ’ вӮ№37,000
```
