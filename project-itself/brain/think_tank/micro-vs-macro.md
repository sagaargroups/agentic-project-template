# 🔬 Micro vs Macro: The Execution Paradox

> The best operators zoom in for precision and zoom out for direction — **in the same day.**

## The Two Lenses

### 🔬 Micro (Daily Work, Precision, Execution)
- Writing the code. Fixing the bug. Shipping the feature.
- Managing the details: naming conventions, file structure, test coverage.
- This is `2_working/` and `4_tasks-and-logs/` energy.
- **Risk:** Getting trapped here = you become a technician, not a leader.

### 🔭 Macro (Growth Work, Vision, Strategy)
- Where is this project in 6 months? In 2 years?
- What partnerships unlock 10x growth?
- What do we stop doing to focus on what matters?
- This is `think_tank/` and `3_learning/` energy.
- **Risk:** Getting trapped here = you become a dreamer, not a builder.

## The Paradox
The best founders are **both** — they can review a pull request at 2pm and redesign the entire business model at 3pm. They switch lenses like breathing.

| Brain Folder | Lens | Purpose |
