# 🔬 Research & Learning — AI Context

> **Folder Type:** Personal Project Space
> **Audience:** AI Agents

## Purpose
The **intelligence vault** for persistent knowledge accumulation. This folder stores research, competitor analysis, tech evaluations, lessons learned, and any knowledge that compounds over the project's lifetime.

## Allowed Contents
- Competitor analysis reports
- Framework and library evaluation notes
- SEO, GEO, and market research strategies
- Lessons learned from past bugs, failures, and refactors
- Technology comparison matrices
- Conference notes, article summaries, and paper digests

## Operational Rules
- **Actionable Synthesis:** Never dump raw articles. Synthesize into actionable bullet points that agents can quickly parse.
- **References:** Always include links to original sources, documentation, or repositories.
- **Categorization:** Use clear, descriptive filenames (e.g., `nextjs-vs-remix-comparison.md`, not `notes.md`).

## AI Guardrails
- **NEVER** hallucinate facts in this folder. If data is missing, explicitly state it.
- **ALWAYS** cross-reference this folder when proposing a new technology or architectural decision.
- **ALWAYS** date-stamp research entries so the agent knows how fresh the information is.
