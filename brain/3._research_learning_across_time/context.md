# 🔬 Research & Learning Context

> **Folder Purpose:** The intelligence vault. This folder is dedicated to competitor analysis, tech stack evaluations, market research, and long-term strategic learning (e.g., GEO optimization research).

## 1. Allowed Contents
- Competitor breakdown reports (`competitor-analysis.md`)
- Framework and library evaluation notes
- SEO and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) strategies
- Logs of lessons learned from past failures or bug fixes

## 2. Operational Rules
- **Actionable Synthesis:** Don't just dump raw articles here. Synthesize long texts into actionable bullet points that agents can easily parse.
- **References:** Always include links to original sources, documentation, or repositories when storing research.

## 3. AI Guardrails
- **NEVER** hallucinate facts in this folder. If you do not know the answer during a research task, state that data is missing.
- **ALWAYS** cross-reference this folder when proposing a new technology stack for a client brand to ensure it aligns with our latest findings.
