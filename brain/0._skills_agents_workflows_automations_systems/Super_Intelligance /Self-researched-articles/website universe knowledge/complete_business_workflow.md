## PHASE 0: BUILDING YOUR ORGANIZATION INFRASTRUCTURE

### Step 1: Legal Foundation (India-Specific)

**Register Your Business:**

**Option A: Freelancer (Simplest - Start Here)**
- No registration needed initially
- Just need PAN card
- File ITR (Income Tax Return) as "Professional Income"
- **Cost: ₹0**

**Option B: Sole Proprietorship**
- Register firm name (optional)
- Get GST registration (if turnover > ₹20 lakhs/year)
- **Cost: ₹5,000-10,000**

**Option C: Private Limited Company (Later, when earning ₹50L+/year)**
- Full company registration
- Separate legal entity
- **Cost: ₹15,000-25,000**

---

### Step 2: Tax Understanding (Simplified)

**Taxes You MUST Know:**

1. **Income Tax**
   - On your profit (income minus expenses)
   - File ITR once a year (July for previous year)
   - Use: New tax regime (easier) or Old regime (more deductions)
   - **Who handles**: You or CA (Chartered Accountant)

2. **GST (Goods & Services Tax)**
   - Only if turnover > ₹20 lakhs/year (₹10 lakhs for some states)
   - 18% GST on services (website development)
   - File monthly/quarterly returns
   - **Zoho Books** handles GST calculations automatically!

3. **TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)**
   - If client is company, they may deduct 10% TDS
   - You get TDS certificate (Form 16A)
   - Adjust this against your final tax
   - **Important**: Always ask for TDS certificate!

**Simple Rule:**
- **First year earning < ₹20L**: Just Income Tax (ITR filing)
- **Earning > ₹20L**: Get GST registration + file GST returns
- **Hire CA**: When earning ₹5L+/year (costs ₹10,000-30,000/year)

**Zoho Books Integration:**
- Auto-calculates GST on invoices
- Generates GST-compliant invoices
- Helps with ITR filing (exports reports for CA)
