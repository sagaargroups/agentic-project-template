## Voice & Tone

### We Sound Like
- Confident but not arrogant
- Knowledgeable but not academic
- Friendly but not unprofessional
- Direct but not pushy

### We NEVER
- Use jargon without explaining it
- Talk down to the audience
- Make false promises
- Use generic corporate speak

### Vocabulary
| Instead of... | We say... |
|---|---|
| "Utilize" | "Use" |
| "Leverage" | "Build on" |
| "Synergy" | "Work together" |
| "Disrupt" | "Change" |
| "Revolutionary" | "Better" or be specific |
