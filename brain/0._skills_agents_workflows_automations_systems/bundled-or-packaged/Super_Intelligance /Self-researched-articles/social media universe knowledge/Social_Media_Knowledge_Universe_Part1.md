**CONTENT FORMATS - TECHNICAL SPECS**

| Format | Specifications | Best Practices |
|--------|---------------|----------------|
| **Feed Posts (Images)** | Square: 1080x1080px (1:1), Portrait: 1080x1350px (4:5), Landscape: 1080x566px (1.91:1) | Use 4:5 for maximum screen real estate, high-quality visuals essential |
| **Feed Videos** | Up to 60 minutes, recommended 15-30 seconds, 1080x1350px (4:5) preferred | First 3 seconds critical, captions essential (80% watch without sound) |
| **Reels** | 90 seconds max, 1080x1920px (9:16), optimal 15-30 seconds | Trending audio crucial, hook in first 1 second, vertical format only |
| **Stories** | 1080x1920px (9:16), 24-hour lifespan, unlimited posts | Use interactive stickers (polls, questions, quizzes), 15-sec video segments |
| **IGTV (Being phased)** | Long-form video (being merged into main feed), up to 60 minutes | Focus on Reels instead, IGTV being deprecated |
| **Live** | 1080x1920px, up to 4 hours, co-streaming (up to 4 people) | Announce ahead, engage in real-time, save to feed after |
| **Carousels** | Up to 10 images/videos, swipeable | Last slide should be CTA, all same aspect ratio, #1 & #2 most important |
| **Guides** | Curated collections of posts | Good for tutorials, recommendations, evergreen content |

**CAPTION SPECS**
- Character limit: 2,200 characters
- Visible without "more": First 125 characters (critical for hook)
- Hashtags: Up to 30 (but 3-5 recommended for quality over quantity in 2025-2026)
- @mentions: Tag people/brands (notifies them, increases reach)
- Line breaks: Use for readability (period/dot method or app)

**ALGORITHM DEEP DIVE (2025-2026)**

**Ranking Factors (in priority order):**
1. **Relationship signals** - How often user interacts with your account (DMs, comments, profile visits, shares)
2. **Interest signals** - Does user engage with similar content types
3. **Timeliness** - Recency of post (fresh content prioritized)
4. **Engagement velocity** - How quickly post gets engagement after publishing
5. **Saves & Shares** - Highest weighted engagement actions (more than likes/comments)
6. **Completion rate (Reels)** - Do people watch to the end? Rewatch?
7. **Audio use (Reels)** - Using trending/popular audio boosts discovery
8. **Original content** - Instagram prioritizes original over reposted (TikTok watermarks penalized)
9. **Consistency** - Regular posting schedule favored

**What Gets Prioritized:**
- Original Reels with trending audio and high completion rates
- Content that sparks conversations (comments, DMs)
- Saves (users bookmarking for later - highest intent signal)
- Shares to Stories/DMs (extends reach)
- Consistent posting (daily optimal, minimum 3-4x/week)
- Strong engagement in first 60 minutes (critical window)

**What Gets Penalized:**
- Reposted content with watermarks (TikTok logo = major penalty)
- Low-quality resolution, pixelated images
- Clickbait captions or engagement bait ("Like if you agree", "Tag 3 friends")
- Buying followers, likes, comments (detected and suppressed)
- External links in captions (use link in bio instead)
- Inconsistent posting (long gaps)
- Spam behavior (too many hashtags, repeated comments)
